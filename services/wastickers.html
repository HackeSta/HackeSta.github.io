What Whatsapp says about Stickers?

If you would like to design your own stickers for WhatsApp, you can package them in an Android app. You will need to distribute your app via the Google Play Store or another mechanism. Users who download and install your sticker app will be able to add your stickers to their WhatsApp sticker picker/tray, and start sending those stickers from within WhatsApp. A separate app is necessary and it will reside on your phone's home screen just like any other app. Stickers on WhatsApp must be legal, authorized, and acceptable. Learn more about acceptable use of our services at https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/#terms-of-service.

What are we offering?

You design your stickers, we make your app.

Note: We don't make Sticker Apps for iOS/Apple App Store, as they don't allow publishing of such apps

Details

We recommend you refer to the FAQ at https://faq.whatsapp.com/general/26000226 for complete details on how to properly create your sticker art. This FAQ also contains a sample PSD that demonstrates best practices for composing legible, rich sticker art.

  • A sticker is an image that has a transparent background and can be sent in a WhatsApp chat
  • Stickers are organized into "packs". Your app can contain anywhere from 1 to 10 packs. Users must explicitly add each pack to WhatsApp one-by-one, so your app should list each pack separately and each pack must have its own affordance to add it to WhatsApp
  • Each sticker pack must have a minimum of 3 stickers and a maximum of 30 stickers
  • Stickers must be exactly 512 x 512 pixels
  • Stickers must be exactly 512 x 512 pixels
  • Sticker Picker/Tray Icon: Provide an image that will be used to represent your sticker pack in the WhatsApp sticker picker/tray. This image should be 96 x 96 pixels

Publishing

  • As Part of Another App

    If you want to make a Sticker or a Sticker Pack, we can add it to one of our own Sticker Apps, with appropriate attribution.

  • A Sticker App on our Play Store Account

    If you want to have a complete Sticker App, we offer to make it and host it on our own Play Store Account with appropriate attribution.

  • A Sticker App on your Play Store Account

    If you want to have your own Play Store Account to host your Sticker App, we offer to either manage it for you, or give you complete control over the account, and host the app on it. Additional charges for creating the account would be required.
